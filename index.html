Stratagem by GDA

The gaming sector has surpassed both the music and film industries in size, and it is still expanding with more than two billion gamers in the world. That is 26% of all people on the planet! The GDA-Game Development Association of Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering works towards providing the right opportunities and environment for passionate Game Developers!

Our council brings to you, STRATAGEM, our annual Game Development Hackathon for the enthusiastic Programmers, Game Developers, Science Freaks And The Tech-Savvy Brains ready to face this 36-hour challenge. The Games developed in this Game Jam are strictly digital not analog. Bring together your team, your tools and whatever it takes to stay awake and win! Can your Stratagems beat ours to become the ultimate Techno-strategist?
